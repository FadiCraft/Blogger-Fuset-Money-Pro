Garmin has announced its first screen-free fitness tracker called the CIRQA Smart Band. Unlike similar alternatives in the market, Garmin’s offering does not require a subscription to access advanced fitness and wellness features.

The Garmin CIRQA Smart Band is claimed to deliver up to 10 days of battery life from a single charge even with 24/7 health monitoring.

It features a fiber-reinforced polymer case with a fabric strap and can be worn around the wrist or the arm. The module weighs 14.8 grams and gets a 5ATM rating.

As for health and fitness tracking, the smart band supports around 80 activities including running, walking, swimming, and more. There’s also a side button available on the module that can be assigned to an activity.

The fitness band can also track sleep, VO2 max, HRV Status, Pulse Oxygen, stress, skin temperature, and more. There’s also Body Battery energy monitoring available and all the data can be accessed via the Garmin Connect app.

Garmin’s CIRQA Smart Band is priced at $199.99 and is currently available for purchase in Black, Citron Gray, Captain Blue, Dark Olive, French Gray, French Blue, and Mauve colored straps.
