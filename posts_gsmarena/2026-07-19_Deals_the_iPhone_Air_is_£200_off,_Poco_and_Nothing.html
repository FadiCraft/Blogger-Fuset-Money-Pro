Last week, we looked at some iPhone 17e and 17 Pro Max discounts – now let’s have a peek at a different model, the iPhone Air.

The phone is one of the thinnest around, measuring 5.6mm and weighing only 165g. It has a titanium frame to keep it tough despite its slender build. The phone has the powerful Apple A19 Pro chipset and a 6.5” LTPO display. What it sacrifices is most of its camera hardware – there’s only a 48MP (1/1.56”) unit on the back – and its battery capacity. The small 3,149mAh battery posted an average endurance with an Active Use Score of 12:44h.
