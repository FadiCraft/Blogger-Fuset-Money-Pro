A new leak suggests Oppo is gearing up to launch its A7 series of smartphones. The leak also reveals the key specifications of one of the lineup's rumored models, the Oppo A7 Pro Max.

Tipster Digital Chat Station shared the alleged specs of the Oppo A7 Pro Max in a post on Weibo. The phone is tipped to be Oppo's first to pack a 10,000mAh battery, corroborating an earlier leak from June.

Oppo A6 Pro

According to the leak, the Oppo A7 Pro Max will sport a 6.78-inch flat AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and 1.5K resolution.

The smartphone is tipped to be powered by the Snapdragon 4 Gen 5 chipset and pack a 10,000mAh battery with support for 80W fast charging. Despite the large battery, it is said to weigh 226g and measure 8.47mm thick.

As for the cameras, the Oppo A7 Pro Max is said to feature a dual rear camera setup comprising a 50MP primary sensor and a 2MP secondary camera. On the front, it is expected to sport a 50MP selfie camera.
