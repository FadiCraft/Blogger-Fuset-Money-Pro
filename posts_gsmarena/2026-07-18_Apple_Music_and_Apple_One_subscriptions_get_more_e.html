Apple has increased the prices of Apple Music and Apple One subscriptions in the US and other countries.

In the US, the individual Apple Music plan is up from $10.99 to $11.99, while the student and family plans now cost $6.99 (up from $5.99) and $19.99 (up from $16.99), respectively.

The price of the individual Apple One plan remains unchanged at $19.95. But the family and premier plans got costlier by $2, as they are now priced at $27.95 and $39.95, respectively.

Commenting on the price hike, Apple said, “As a result of rising licensing costs, Apple Music is increasing its subscription price beginning today.”
