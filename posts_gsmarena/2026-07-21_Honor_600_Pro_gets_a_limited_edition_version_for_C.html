For ten days only, fans of actress Wang Churan can pick up a limited edition version of the Honor 600 Pro for China. The promo will run from July 21 to 31 and is available through authorized partner stores in the country.


Honor 600 Pro (China) Flash Limited Edition Gift Box

The “Flash Limited Edition Gift Box” includes the phone in Sky Blue color, a quicksand phone case, a 10,000mAh power bank, plus extra goodies like a tote bag, limited edition stickers and a Wang Churan flash card.

The Honor 600 Pro 12/256GB is available with a CNY 200 discount on its regular price, so it sells for CNY 3,700 ($545 / €480 / ₹52,500). Additionally, Honor is offering free Earbuds X9 (valued at CNY 300). You can get a trade-in discount of up to CNY 1,000 and split the purchase into 24 interest-free monthly installments.


Honor 600 Pro colors

Again, this promo is available only for China and note that the Chinese Honor 600 Pro differs from the global model.
