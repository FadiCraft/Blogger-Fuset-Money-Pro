iQOO has officially launched the Z11 Lite in India after revealing nearly every aspect of the smartphone ahead of its debut, leaving only the pricing under wraps.

The iQOO Z11 Lite is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset, paired with up to 6GB of RAM and up to 256GB of onboard storage. The handset also includes a microSD card slot for further storage expansion.

Up front, it features a 6.74-inch HD+ LCD display with a 120Hz refresh rate and up to 1,200 nits of peak brightness.

For photography, the phone gets a 50MP Sony IMX852 primary camera, accompanied by a 0.08MP monochrome sensor. On the front, a 5MP selfie camera is housed inside a waterdrop notch.

The rear panel also incorporates a multi-color notification light. Meanwhile, keeping the lights on is a 6,500mAh battery with support for 44W fast charging.

The iQOO Z11 Lite ships with OriginOS 6 and is promised 2 years of Android OS updates along with 4 years of security patches. Other features include a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, an IP65 rating, 5G, dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.4, and GPS.

The smartphone is offered in Midnight Blue and Solar Flame color options and will go on sale starting July 30.

iQOO Z11 Lite price in India starts at INR 19,499 ($205) for the 4GB/128GB option, while the 6GB/128GB and 6GB/256GB variants cost INR 21,499 ($225) and INR 24,499 ($255), respectively.
