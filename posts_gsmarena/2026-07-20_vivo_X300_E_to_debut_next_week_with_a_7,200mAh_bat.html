After weeks of rumors, vivo has officially confirmed the X300 E. The smartphone will be available for purchase in China next week. vivo has also revealed its design, color options, and a few key specs.

The vivo X300 E will go on sale in China on July 27, with pre-orders already live through the company's official online store.

The smartphone will be available in black, orange, and white color options. It sports a square camera module housing a Zeiss-branded triple rear camera setup, including a periscope telephoto lens.

vivo has also confirmed that the X300 E will pack a 7,200mAh battery. Other specifications are yet to be officially revealed.

Previous leaks suggest the vivo X300 E will feature a 6.59-inch AMOLED display and a Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset. It is expected to offer a 50MP primary camera, a 50MP periscope telephoto camera, and an 8MP ultra-wide sensor.

The handset is also rumored to support 90W wired fast charging and weigh around 203g.
