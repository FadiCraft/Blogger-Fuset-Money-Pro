According to new information from a tipster on X, Oppo is currently testing the upcoming Find X10, Find X10 Pro, and Find X10 Pro Max alongside the iPhone 17 Pro, presumably to compare their camera prowess against Apple’s latest flagship.

The images below seem to show all three devices in a rig with the iPhone 17 Pro, and even though the Oppos are inside a case that's meant to obscure their designs, we can make out the fact that they all appear to have an iPhone-like two-part rear panel design. Or, to put it another way, an iPhone-like gigantic rectangular camera island that stretches across the entire width of the devices' backs.


Oppo Find X10 family spotted alongside the iPhone 17 Pro

We can't be sure if this is the same Oppo device three times or three different ones, though, since they seem to be positioned differently in the rig - the one in the middle is noticeably higher than the one next to the iPhone, for example. Anyway, we assume the entire family will have the same design as that's what makes the most sense.

Oppo is expected to unveil this trio in September, and if that pans out then we should see a lot more of them in the coming weeks.
