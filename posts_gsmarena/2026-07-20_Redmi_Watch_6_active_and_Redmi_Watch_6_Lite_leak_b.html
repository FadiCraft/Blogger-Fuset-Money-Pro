According to a new leak, Redmi is currently working on two smartwatches - the Redmi Watch 6 Active and the Redmi Watch 6 Lite. You can see both in the leaked images below. These will complement the Redmi Watch 6 in the brand's portfolio.

Both of them will come with over 140 sports modes, heart rate and SpO2 monitoring, sleep tracking, and of course distance tracking. Interestingly, despite what the names might imply, the Redmi Watch 6 Active will be cheaper than the Redmi Watch 6 Lite, launching at €50 in the Eurozone, while the Lite model will go for €60.


Redmi Watch 6 Active leaked renders

The Redmi Watch 6 Active will have a 1.85-inch AMOLED screen with a 390x450 resolution, Bluetooth 5.3, and a 470 mAh battery. The Redmi Watch 6 Lite will have a 1.96-inch AMOLED screen with 410x502 resolution, a 9-axis motion sensor, multi-system GPS, and a speaker. It has the same 470 mAh battery as the Active.


Redmi Watch 6 Lite leaked renders

It's still unclear when these are going to launch, as well as where - Europe is a given since the leaked prices are in euros, but we're not sure where else you'll be able to buy them. Stay tuned and we'll let you know when we find out more.
