Last week, nubia unveiled the NaviX Ultra in China, but only revealed its design and color options and kept its hardware details under wraps. However, a tipster has now leaked the smartphone's key specifications.

In a now-deleted post, tipster Bald Panda claimed that the nubia NaviX Ultra will be powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset.

According to the tipster, the handset will feature a 6.78-inch display with a 1.5K resolution and a refresh rate of 144Hz.

The NaviX Ultra is also tipped to offer a triple 50MP rear camera setup, a 50MP front-facing camera, and a 7,100mAh battery with support for 80W fast charging.

nubia is calling the NaviX Ultra as the world's first AI-agent smartphone. It is confirmed to run the Doubao AI assistant and will be offered in four color options at launch.
