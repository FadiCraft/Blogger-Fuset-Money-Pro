                                  If it was done in performance mode then that means it's possible that the battery life test could be significantly higher.

                                  Also people talk about how there is no improvement from the V5 and in a way it's true, but it is of most phones with small marginal improvements. But I would say that the changes aren't small at all especially when you compare what they did compared to other folds... although how it all works in the end doesn't necessarily mean better.

                                  I mean they not only made it slimmer, but they went from under 6000Mah in the V5 to up to 7150mah. that is mind blowing (Of course you have to get the China version which many feel is actually the more desirable OS to get). Now that's another thing where every reviewer keeps claiming the battery in the V6 has like 30% more silicone but that is obviously ONLY for the special blade batteries in the 1TB Chinese model.

                                  They also apparently made the camera island thinner and stick out less, I haven't' seen anyone measure it though, would be interesting to see. So they made the camera island thinner while keeping the same quality BUT ALSO adding super stabilization with CIPA certification to the zoom camera.

                                  They also
                                  -made the front screen bigger
                                  -somehow managed to include what they say is a big vapor chamber
                                  -Bar phone level water resistance
                                  -Claim to have made a super strong hinge
                                  -went full snapdragon chip.

                                  So potentially if you put a cooler on it like some of the gaming phones do, you'd be able to get the full on gaming emulation beast that this chip is known for.
