Samsung is set to host its Galaxy Unpacked event on Wednesday, July 22, in London, UK. The company is expected to unveil its next-generation Galaxy Z foldables, including a new Galaxy Z Fold model with a wide form-factor, alongside the latest Galaxy Watch lineup.

The event will be livestreamed on Samsung's official website and YouTube channel, starting at 9:00 AM EDT, 3:00 PM CET, 6:30 PM IST, and 1:00 PM UTC. You can also watch the keynote live using the embedded stream below.

Want to know what Samsung has in store for Galaxy Unpacked? Check out our detailed roundup of everything that's expected to be announced at the event.

One of the biggest announcements is expected to be Samsung's rumored wide-screen Galaxy Z Fold8, which is set to introduce a new foldable form factor. The device is expected to take on Apple's rumored iPhone Ultra and Huawei's existing Pura X Max.

Samsung is also expected to unveil the Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra, the successor to the Galaxy Z Fold7, and the Galaxy Z Flip8. Meanwhile, the company will also announce the Galaxy Watch 9 lineup and the Galaxy Watch Ultra 2.
