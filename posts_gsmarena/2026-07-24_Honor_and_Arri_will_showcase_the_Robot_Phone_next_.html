The Honor Robot Phone may arrive earlier than expected – Honor announced an “Imaging Technology Launch Event” for July 28 (that’s Tuesday next week). Honor, along with partner Arri, will present a phone that “packs a full-stack cinematic workflow”.

Honor and Arri will hold an Imaging Technology Launch Event on the 28th

They are really leaning into the phone’s video skills. If you watched the FIFA World Cup 2026 final, you may have spotted the Robot Phone. It also made an appearance at the Shanghai International Film Festival. And even though it was first unveiled months ago, the complete spec sheet of the device is still kept under wraps.

While all this is happening, Honor refreshed its brand with a new logo – a split ring. This comes with a new tagline too: “Dare To Be”. Below you will find a video that introduces the new logo and explains what it means.
