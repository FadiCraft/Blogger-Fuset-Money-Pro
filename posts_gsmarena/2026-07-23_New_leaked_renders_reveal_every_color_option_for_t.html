We're just weeks away from Google's Pixel 11 series launch, and by now, almost everything about the upcoming lineup has leaked. A fresh leak has now revealed the complete color lineup for the Pixel 11, Pixel 11 Pro, and Pixel 11 Pro XL.

The latest renders, shared by MyMobiles in collaboration with OnLeaks, reveal that the Pixel 11 and Pixel 11 Pro phones will each be offered in four color options.


The standard Pixel 11 is said to arrive in Fuchsia, Light Sterling, Moss, and Midnight Haze. Fuchsia appears to be a vibrant pink finish, making it the boldest color option in the lineup.

Meanwhile, the Pixel 11 Pro and Pixel 11 Pro XL are tipped to launch in Dune, Light Fog, Midnight Haze, and Pine colorways.


The Dune and Pine variants of the Pixel 11 Pro and Pixel 11 Pro XL appear to feature glossy, color-matched frames. The Dune model is paired with a champagne-finished frame, while the Pine variant gets a polished gold frame.

Google is set to unveil the Pixel 11 lineup, including the Pixel 11 Pro Fold, at its Made by Google event on August 12. The upcoming phones are expected to be powered by the new 2nm Tensor G6 chipset, feature triple rear cameras, and introduce a new Pixel Glow LED notification system on the back.

The standard Pixel 11 and Pixel 11 Pro are tipped to sport 6.3-inch displays, while the Pixel 11 Pro XL is expected to feature a larger 6.8-inch screen.
