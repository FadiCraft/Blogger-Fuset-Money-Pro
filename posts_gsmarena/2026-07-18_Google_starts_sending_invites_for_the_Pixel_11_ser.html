Google sent invites to the media in the US last week for the Pixel 11 series launch event, which will be held in New York City on August 12 at 6 PM ET. Now, the Mountain View-based tech giant has sent out invites in India as well.

The Google Pixel 11 series launch event in India will be hosted on August 13 in Gurugram. The invite doesn't mention when the event will begin, but it could start at 12 PM local time, after which we'll receive details on pricing and availability for the Pixel 11 series in India.

Google is yet to make any announcements regarding the Pixel 11 series launch event in India on its official social media accounts and Indian website; however, its online India store says pre-orders for the Pixel 11 series will begin on August 12, and those who sign up by August 8, 12:29 PM local time will receive an exclusive offer on eligible Pixel Buds or Pixel Watch when buying them with a new Pixel 11 series phone.

Google Store (India)

The Pixel 11 series reportedly includes four smartphones - the Pixel 11, the Pixel 11 Pro, the Pixel 11 Pro XL, and the Pixel 11 Pro Fold. Whether or not Google launches all models in India remains to be seen. Google launched their predecessors in India last year, so we doubt the internet search giant will skip any model this time.
