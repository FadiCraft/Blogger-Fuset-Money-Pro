At this point, we've seen and we've heard it all. The Galaxy Z Fold8, Z Fold8 Ultra and Galaxy Watch9 leaks we've seen so far leave nothing to the imagination. We've already seen some renders and most of the specs.


Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra

However, the latest leak comes from a reputable source, Evan Blass, and by the looks of it, all the renders are official. This, in turn, means that all the features and specs you see in the images are basically confirmed.


Galaxy Z Fold8

There are no surprises when it comes to the camera hardware on the Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra - it offers the same hardware as last year's Galaxy Z Fold7. The regular Z Fold8's 50MP+50MP setup on the back is also confirmed. The updated battery capacities seem to be final, too.

The Galaxy Watch9 renders, on the other hand, aren't all that revealing, but we see the Snapdragon Wear Elite chip being mentioned as well as some fitness and health-tracking features that we've already seen in the previous models.

Is there anything left to unpack at the upcoming Galaxy Unpacked event scheduled for July 22? Hopefully the answer is yes.
